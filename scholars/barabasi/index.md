title: Albert-László Barabási
---

Author of the Network Science book. 
Pioneered discoveries in network science and network medicine.
Discovered the concept of scale-free networks and proposed the Barabási–Albert model, which explains the widespread emergence of such networks in natural, technological and social systems, including the World Wide Web and online communities. 


- Network Science Book: [link](https://networksciencebook.com/)
- Emergence of scaling in random networks: [Science '99](https://www.science.org/doi/abs/10.1126/science.286.5439.509)
